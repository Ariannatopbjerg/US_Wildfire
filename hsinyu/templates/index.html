Wildfire Interactive Visualizations

Living in California while fire was rampant, it sparked our interest to analyze wildfires across the United States from 1992-2015. We utilized Random Forest Machine Learning Model to predict the class of the fire size in an effort to educate the public and for resources distribution. Here you will find an interactive table and visualizations pertaining to wildfires across the United States.







Please click to display fire data from 1992-2015 of the state and/or county of interest.



Select year to view fires for that year.
